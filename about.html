We may not be able to get every country on this globe to participate, but even if we get the majority of the larger countries, it could be successful.


Dollars for Peace aims to do just that by initiating a new movement to create a worldwide military police force that will possess the power to suppress any aggressive invasion or violence that threatens the peace of a country. Also, allow those who truly believe in a more peaceful world to make extra income by urging others to contribute to our efforts. Any country can obtain the protection of the worldwide police force, but they must be part of this force and they must give free rights to their citizens to choose a representative government of their choice.
